Prefix Key: CTRL + a

  # Session, window, pane management
    t       New window
    n       Next window
    ,       Rename window
    h       Horizontal pane
    g       Vertical pane
    w       Close pane
    q       Kill session

    K      resize pane up
    J      resize pane down
    H      resize pane left
    L      resize pane right

  # Navigating cursor
    C-a     Jump beginning of the line
    C-e     Jump end of the line
